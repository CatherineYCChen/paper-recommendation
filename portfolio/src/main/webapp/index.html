About Me: Background

I was born in Taipei, Taiwan in 2000. Along with my mother, and sister, I immigrated to Toronto, Canada in 2010, while my father stayed in Taiwan to work and support our family.

I studied in Canada from grade 5 to 12, and I attended high school at Earl Haig Secondary School, where I was part of the Claude Watson Arts Program , as a music major, specifically for the cello.

My Music Experience

I began my musical journey by playing piano in kindergarden.

Afterwards, my elementary school required that the students participate in some sort of extra-curriculum activity, and I experimented with many things. Ultimately, I began playing the cello seriously when I was 10 years old and continued until I was a sophomore in high school. During my junior and senior years of high school, I focused more on chamber and orchestral music, as well as composition
